Week 01 game plan (plain-English version)

If you have never seen a game design doc before: this section explains, in simple terms, how the game works and why each weekly release matters—now told like a briefing comic, not a textbook.

Panel A • Vocab cheat sheet
Notes
  • Core loop: the repeatable sequence you do every run.
  • Hero kit: the player’s move set and abilities.
  • Faction: a family of enemies with a shared combat style.
  • Mutator: a weekly gameplay rule that changes how you play.
Panel B • The pitch (3 beats)
Beat 1

Rift Courier is an action game where you jump into dangerous zones, recover rare fragments, and use them to rebuild your home base.

Beat 2
Breach Route Recover Extract Rebuild
Those five verbs are the player fantasy in motion.
Beat 3
  • Combat is clear and responsive.
  • Your decisions change risk and rewards.
  • Your base visibly grows over time.
  • Each week adds a meaningful new twist.
How one run works (taught panel-by-panel)

Follow my countdown—this is the same loop we prototype in Week 01.

1Pick a mission—easy warm-up or spicy route.

2Fight room-to-room: movement and timing matter more than stats.

3Grab the main fragment (and bonus loot if you’re greedy).

4Make the risk call: extract safe or push for bigger prizes.

5Spend earnings to unlock stronger missions back at base.
